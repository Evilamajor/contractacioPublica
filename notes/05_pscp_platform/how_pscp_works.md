# PSCP (Public Procurement Portal of Catalonia)

## 1. What PSCP Offers
- publication of tenders,
- submission of bids,
- access to award decisions.

## 2. Key Sections
- Contractor Profile
- Tender Finder
- Announcements and Documentation
- Electronic Submission

## 3. Relation With the Simulator
The Mini-PSCP simulator will replicate:
- contract creation,
- bid submission,
- award computation on synthetic data.
