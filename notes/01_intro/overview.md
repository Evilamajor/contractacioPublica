# Introduction to Public Procurement

## Purpose of These Notes
These notes compile the core concepts of public procurement in Catalonia and Spain, combining:
- theoretical explanations,
- examples,
- practical exercises,
- and the development of a Mini-PSCP simulator.

## What You Will Learn
- Legal foundations of public sector procurement
- Main actors and responsibilities
- Contract types and procedures
- How the PSCP platform works
- How to analyze procurement data

## Connection with the Mini-PSCP Simulator
All theoretical knowledge will be applied practically through the simulator:
- contract creation
- bids
- evaluation
- award simulation
