# Types of Procurement Procedures

## 1. Open Procedure
Any company can submit a bid.

## 2. Restricted Procedure
Only selected companies can submit bids.

## 3. Negotiated Procedure
Used when:
- no bidders in an open procedure,
- technical or artistic reasons,
- extreme urgency.

## 4. Minor Contracts
Simplified procedure for small amounts.

## 5. Dynamic Purchasing System
Digital, multi-supplier, long-term tool.
