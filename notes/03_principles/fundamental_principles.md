# Fundamental Principles of Public Procurement

## 1. Equality of Treatment
All bidders must receive:
- same information,
- same deadlines,
- same conditions.

## 2. Transparency
- publication on the PSCP,
- access to documentation,
- traceability of award decisions.

## 3. Competition
- avoid unnecessary solvency requirements,
- avoid discriminatory technical specifications.

## 4. Publicity
- correct publication of procedures,
- clear and complete tender documents.

## 5. Efficiency
- use public funds responsibly,
- evaluate lifecycle cost,
- justify needs properly.
